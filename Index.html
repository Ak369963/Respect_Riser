Why Students Should Not Misbehave

Teachers and parents shape your future. Misbehaving builds walls. Respect opens doors to opportunity and trust. Disrespect leads to regret.

Control Your Anger

  • Take deep breaths
  • Walk away and count to 20
  • Write down your feelings
  • Listen before reacting
  • Use calm words
  • Think about consequences
  • Talk to someone you trust

Benefits of Respectful Behavior

- Stronger relationships
- More trust from teachers
- Better school and home life
- Improved self-esteem
- Positive reputation
- Leadership opportunities

Tips to Build Respect

  • Say please and thank you
  • Be honest and kind
  • Apologize if wrong
  • Help others
  • Stay calm during arguments
  • Give full attention when someone speaks
